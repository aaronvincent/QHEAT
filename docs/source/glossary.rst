Glossary of Terms
=================

Astrophysics
------------

.. _m-200:

:math:`M_{200}`
***************
The mass of the galaxy enclosed in :math:`R_{200}` (R-200_).


.. _r-200:

:math:`R_{200}`
***************
The radius of a galaxy in which the average density
is 200 times the critical density of the universe.
